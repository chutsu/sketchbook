QR Decomposition

QR decomposition or QR factorization is a decomposition of a matrix

$\mat{A}$ into a product,

$$ \mat{A} = \mat{Q} \mat{R} $$

of an orthogonal matrix $\mat{Q}$ and an upper triangular matrix

$\mat{R}$. If $\mat{A}$ is square and invertible, then the

factorization is unique if we require the diagonal elements of $\mat{R}$

to be positive.
